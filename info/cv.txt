SECTION: Research Experience

ITEM: Texas A&M University
META: Graduate Assistant-Research • Sep 2021 - Current
DETAIL: Action Planning of a Laboratory Gantry Robot using Large Language Model [WIP]: natural language-based task planning for a 5-DOF gantry robot that converts high-level instructions into task-level actions and low-level control parameters.
DETAIL: FABRIC: an end-to-end learning-from-demonstration toolkit for low-cost fabrication and kinematic programming of bodily-expressive robots, enabling accessible sign language synthesis and embodied interaction.
DETAIL: Emotions Amplified: an AI-driven system that generates emotionally expressive head gestures for sign language avatars using imitation learning and reinforcement learning.
DETAIL: End-User Reconfiguration of Environment: an agentic AI framework for generative fabrication that interprets multimodal inputs and proposes explainable, fabrication-ready augmentations.
DETAIL: Underground Metal Pipe Detection and Haptic Feedback: a wireless sensing and feedback system for excavation safety using IMU and magnetometer sensing.
DETAIL: Ubiquitous Microvibration Harvesters: low-cost 3D-printed harvesters for self-powered, context-aware sensing.
DETAIL: RLScara: reinforcement learning-based control of a SCARA manipulator with both simulated and 3D-printed physical validation.
DETAIL: Interaction Energy Harvesting: a toolkit that lets non-experts fabricate mechanisms that convert everyday interactions into electrical energy.
DETAIL: Robotizing Everyday Objects: an end-to-end system for turning ordinary objects into mobile robotic devices using 3D-printed attachments.
